Summary

I am currently a PhD candidate in mechanical engineering at MIT. I work in the Device Realization Lab with PI Dr. Brian Anthony.

My research is focused on low-cost volume ultrasound methods. My other research interests include medical devices, medical ultrasound, translational research, engineering education, and participatory design.

This research is on-going - keep an eye on this page for updates!
