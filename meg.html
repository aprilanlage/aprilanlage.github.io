MEG chair

Summary

In 2.75 (Medical Device Design) in Fall 2019, I worked with a team of 5 engineers to design and build a device that could comfortabley allow patients to enter a MEG machine and sit for the duration of a scan.

Problem

The problem was posed by Sumitomo Heavy Industries (SHI), which had built a magnetoencephalography (MEG) machine The MEG machine cannot move, or it needs to be re-calibrated. The MEG machine is designed to fit the height of a standard room, which leaves little space for a person to maneuver themselves inside for a scan.

Our challenge was to create a system that allowed a patient to get into and out of the MEG machine and be comfortable inside the machine for the duration of the scan.
