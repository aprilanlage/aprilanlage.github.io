MEG chair

Summary

In 2.75 (Medical Device Design) in Fall 2019, I worked with a team of 5 engineers to design and build a device that could comfortabley allow patients to enter a MEG machine and sit for the duration of a scan.

Problem

The MEG machine cannot move, or it needs to be re-calibrated.
