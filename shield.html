Baseball cap face shield

Summary

As a teaching assistant for 2.00b (Toy Product Design), I was tasked with leading the design and fabrication of face shields for ~100 students. The shield needed to be shop-safe. Additionally, it had to be comfortable enough for students to wear during a weekly 3-hour lab section.
This was a re-design of the shield I had designed and made in the pervious semester.

Ultimately, with the help of the other class TA's, more than 120 face shields and clips were manufactured and distributed to students and teaching staff. The design was considered more comfortable and practical than the previous semester's design. Many students

Key Skills

  • Rapid prototyping - 3D printing (FDM, PolyJet) and laser cutting
  • Design process - idea generation to manufacturing
